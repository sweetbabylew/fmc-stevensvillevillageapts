Where we are

The island setting is calm and scenic, and the Bay Bridge puts the mainland’s shopping, medical care, and services only minutes away. Nearby Kent Island, Kent Narrows, and the historic towns of Queenstown, Centreville, and Easton offer waterfront dining, small shops, and local history, and the famous Wye Oak is a short drive.

Getting around

The surrounding area is easy to enjoy at an unhurried pace, with a pharmacy, grocery, the county library, and the AAMC health center all just a few minutes away. When residents want to reach farther, Salisbury, Maryland, and Dover, Delaware, are each about an hour away.
