The Neighborhood

Life around Chester

Stevensville Village Apartments sits in a quiet part of Chester, close to the shopping, dining, and everyday places you need. Stevensville Village is a quiet community for seniors on Kent Island, in Queen Anne’s County, just across the Chesapeake Bay Bridge from the Annapolis side.
